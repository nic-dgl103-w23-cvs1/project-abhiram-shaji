Frequently asked questions

How much do Lars Tornøe's products typically cost?

Our products vary in price depending on factors such as size, complexity, and materials used. We work with a range of budgets, and are committed to providing our clients with high-quality, beautiful products at fair prices.

What types of products does Lars Tornøe design?

At Lars Tornøe, we specialize in furniture design and related fields of product design. Our products include everything from custom dining tables and seating to lighting fixtures and home accessories.

How does the design process work at Lars Tornøe?

Our design process is collaborative and client-focused. We start by meeting with our clients to discuss their vision and needs, and then work together to create sketches and designs that bring that vision to life. We value communication throughout the process to ensure that the final product meets our clients' expectations.

Does Lars Tornøe ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our products internationally. Shipping fees vary depending on the location and size of the product, so please contact us for more information.

Can I request a custom design from Lars Tornøe?

Absolutely! We love working with our clients to create custom designs that meet their specific needs and preferences. Please reach out to us with your ideas, and we'll work together to create something truly unique and beautiful.

Will I receive an estimate before the design process begins?

Yes, we provide our clients with a detailed estimate before any work begins. We believe in transparency and honesty throughout the design process, and want to ensure that our clients are fully informed about the costs associated with their project.

From Tornøe

At Lars Tornøe, our passion for design is what drives us every day. As the owner of this design studio, I am incredibly proud of the work that we do and the team that we have assembled. Our mission is simple: to create beautiful, functional furniture and products that enhance the spaces in which they exist. We are committed to collaboration and communication, and we work closely with our clients to ensure that their vision is fully realized. We take pride in our Scandinavian heritage and the values of simplicity and craftsmanship that it represents. Above all, we believe that good design can make a positive impact on people's lives, and we are honored to be able to share that belief with our clients. Thank you for considering Lars Tornøe for your design needs, and we look forward to working with you to create something truly special.
