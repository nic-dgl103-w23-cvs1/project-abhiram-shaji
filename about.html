About

Lars Tornøe is an award-winning design studio based in Stavanger, Norway, dedicated to creating innovative furniture and product designs for clients in Scandinavia and the US. Their designs have been recognized with accolades such as the DogA award and Designer of The Year by Bo Bedre Magazine. With a focus on simplicity and functionality, Lars Tornøe's designs are both beautiful and practical. Our studio, located at Kvitsøygata 10A, is an inspiring space where creativity thrives, and their commitment to excellence is evident in every piece they produce. If you're looking for timeless and innovative furniture designs, Lars Tornøe is a design studio worth watching.

Our Mission

At Lars Tornøe, our mission is to create exceptional furniture and product designs that combine functionality with a uniquely Nordic aesthetic. Our design studio, based in Stavanger, Norway, works with a diverse range of clients from Scandinavia and the US, providing innovative solutions to their design needs. Our commitment to excellence has been recognized with numerous accolades, including the prestigious DogA award, Red Dot Award, and Designer of The Year by Bo Bedre Magazine. Our designs are the result of a rigorous creative process that places great emphasis on attention to detail, quality craftsmanship, and sustainability. We believe that our mission of creating outstanding designs is not just about creating beautiful and functional objects but also about making a positive impact on people's lives. Our studio, located at Kvitsøygata 10A, is where we bring our designs to life, and we invite you to join us on this journey of innovation and excellence.
