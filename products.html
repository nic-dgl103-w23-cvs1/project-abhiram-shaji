Products

Kizu

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by New Works

Table lamp with white or black marble base and white acrylic shade. Available in large and small size.

Hex

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Heymat

Industrial quality doormat with a highly functional embossed design. Available in two sizes and two colors.

Tind

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Fora Form

A light sofa for work, meetings and concentration that shares a lot of DNA with the Fjell chair. Available in 2 widths with high or low back. Originally designed for Finansparken in Stavanger.

Coil

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Muuto

Coat rack in extruded aluminium with slidable hooks.

The Dots

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Muuto, Denmark

Coat hooks of solid oak or painted ash wood. The hooks come in several sizes. Available in sets of five or as individual hooks. The original Dots are made in wood. Metal Dots and Ceramic Dots have later been added to the Dots family.

Pinch

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Arven

Pinch are cellars for salt, pepper and other seasonings. The bodies are made from massive brass and plated with pure silver. The heavy weight and precise detailing add an exceptional sense of quality to the objects.

Stripes

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Vestre, Norway

Modular outdoor seating system consisting of two primary modules, a rectangle and a semicircle. The modules can be combined to create benches in many shapes and sizes. They are also available with holes to allow placement around trees.

Scoop

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Piece By Ps

Family of three multi-purpose bowls that can be used to store or display small objects, or simply for decoration. Circular tools are used to carve out depressions from the rectangular blanks. Available in different materials and finishes.

Fjell

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Fora Form

Series of super comfortable conference chairs. Available with backrest in 3 different heights. Optional armrests, 4-star base, 5-star base and 4-legged base.

Bloc

Designed by Atle Tveit and Lars Tornøe | produced by Vestre, Norway

Bloc is a series of benches and waste containers for outdoor environments. The diagonal fold found on all Bloc products adds structural rigidity and provides a visual theme for the series.

Con

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Fora Form

Con is a versatile series of chairs, built around the seat shell. Configuration options include two different backs, four bases and armrests. In addition seat and backs are available in various materials and colors, providing countless possibilities.

Kvart

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Fora Form

Kvart is a series of tables based on a sleek aluminium construction that can be configured as X- and T-base for tables in different sizes, heights and shapes. The name Kvart originates from the column's profile which indicates four directions.

Clover

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Cooee Design

Clover is a simplified interpretation of classical porcelain styles that might bring items from grandmothers kitchen to mind. Arranged in a triplet, the curved surfaces generate a design language that is both botanical and geometric. The ceramic vases are available in four sizes.

Undring

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Figgjo

Figgjo Undring is a low profile plate with a raised hole and accompanying bowls in three sizes. The bowls fit onto the hole and become a part of the plate, allowing chefs to get creative with garnishes, sauces, decorations etc, served à part.

Copenhagen Chair

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Fora Form

Armchair, designed as part of Master´s degree project using the Norwegian Ambassador’s residency in Copenhagen as case. Upholstery on three-dimensional plywood moulding, steel wire base.

Corner

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Vestre, Norway

Corner is a bench designed for hard architectural features, such as low walls and slabs. It wraps around corners and provides a warmer and softer sitting experience.

Clint

Designed by Atle Tveit and Lars Tornøe | produced by Fora Form

Series of conference chairs. Available with high back or low back, 4-star base, 5-star base with gas tilt mechanism and stackable steel wire base. Optional armrests. The Clint series also includes a lower club version of the chair.

Nunu

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Vestre, Norway

Nunu is a series of outdoor benches and tables which combine a modern design with classical references.

Myk

Designed by Lars Tornøe | produced by Fora Form

Table series with soft wood details on both base and table top. Available with X- and T-base, different heights and various table tops.

Bone

Designed by Atle Tveit, Steinar Hindenes and Lars Tornøe | produced by Materia, Sweden

Armchair constructed from one single plywood moulding. Available with visible wood shell or fully upholstered. 4-star steel base or steel wire base. The Bone series is complemented by various tables and a smaller version of the chair.
